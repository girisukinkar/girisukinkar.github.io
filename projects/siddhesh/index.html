Places to wander near Nagaon

Feel the amaizng breeze on the beach

Alibaug Beach

Distance: 10km 
Travel Time: 30mins

The most attractive and common among beaches due to its clean environment and water sports

Kulaba Fort

Distance:- 9.3 km
Travel Time: 27mins

Built by Maratha warrior Shivaji Maharaj in the year 1680, it is a must visit to know about historical battles and ancient cravings and designs
Timing is 10AM to 5PM

Akshi Beach

Distance:- 3.2km
Travel Time: 12mins

If you are finding a place to alienate yourself from the world at the same time wanna enjoy the low-tides and watch different species of birds this is the place where you will find peace and harmony.

Kihim Beach

Distance:- 19.2Km
Travel Time:- 45mins

One of the best romantic place to sit and fall in love with clean enivronment and fresh air also to enjoy water sports such as  Banana Rides, Jet Skiing, Bumper Rides, Kayaking, Parasailing, Speed Boating and Fishing

Raigad Bazaar

Distance:- 8.7 km
Travel Time:- 21mins

In mood of shopping  small items, handicrafts, clothes and accessories? this is the best place in close proximity of Nagaon to visit
